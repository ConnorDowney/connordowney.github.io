Streakz — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

This document describes the Privacy Policy for the Streakz mobile application (the "App") operated by Downey Digital LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and login credentials.
  • Age Confirmation: To verify eligibility (18+).
  • Winner Information (Only if Selected): Full legal name, state of residence, contact information needed to deliver prizes, and tax information if required by law.

We do not collect payment information from users.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • App usage data (e.g., picks submitted, feature usage)
  • Device and technical information (for app functionality and security)

We do not collect precise location or GPS data.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the Streakz app, track picks and eligibility for Promotions, communicate with users (including winner notifications), prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal and tax obligations.

3. Promotions & Prizes

Participation in Streakz Promotions is free and requires no purchase. Additional information is requested only from selected winners and solely for verifying eligibility, delivering prizes, and meeting legal or tax requirements.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers who assist in operating the app, when required by law or legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the Company.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to operate the app, fulfill Promotions, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect user information. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Streakz is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have, contact us to request deletion.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Requests may be submitted by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated effective date.
